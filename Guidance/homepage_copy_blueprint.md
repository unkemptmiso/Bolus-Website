# Bolus Homepage Copy & SEO Blueprint

This blueprint applies the "Human-First" Reddit philosophy. The visible text is ultra-clean, clinical, and punchy to optimize for human conversion. The SEO keywords ("anesthesia record app", "office-based anesthesia charting") are injected using "invisible" methods like semantic HTML and Meta tags.

---

## 0. Invisible SEO (The `<head>` Code)
These elements are read by Google and AI bots, but are invisible on the actual page design.
* **Title Tag (50-60 chars)**: Bolus | Modern Office-Based Anesthesia Record App 
* **Meta Description (150-160 chars)**: Ditch paper charting. Bolus is a clean, mobile-first anesthesia record app designed to help office-based practices create highly defensible documentation.
* **Schema Markup**: `SoftwareApplication` JSON-LD payload injected in the head.

---

## Section 1: The Hero (High Impact Conversion)

* **Invisible H1 (For Screen Readers & Google)**:
  * `<h1 class="sr-only">The Modern Anesthesia Record App for Office-Based Practice</h1>`
* **Visible Display Headline (H2 visually styled massive)**: 
  * *Ditch Paper. Modernize your practice with clean and defensible records.*
* **Subheadline (H3)**: 
  * A clean, mobile-first anesthesia record app built to replace messy paper workflows with a faster and more defensible standard.
* **Primary CTA**: Download on the App Store
* **Secondary CTA (Ghost Button)**: See how it works
* **Visual**: Apple-forward design. Maximal white space, clean SF Pro typography, and an iPad floating with the crisp Bolus interface on screen.

---

## Section 2: The Problem (Agitating the Friction)

**Goal**: Validate their frustration with paper and bulky practice management software.

* **Section Headline (H2)**: 
  * *Your documentation shouldn't feel like a liability.*
* **Body/Grid Items (H3)**:
  * **Messy Handwriting is Risky.**
    * Illegible timestamps and crossed-out vitals create ambiguity when you need clarity the most.
  * **Paper is Slow.**
    * Wasted time scanning, printing, and retrieving charts from dusty filing cabinets.
  * **"All-in-One" EMRs are Bloated.**
    * Most software forces you to tap through billing and scheduling just to chart a medication. Bolus is built purely for clinical documentation.

---

## Section 3: The Solution / Transformation

**Goal**: Show how Bolus solves the pain points with speed and precision.

* **Section Headline (H2)**: 
  * *Charting built for speed. Records built to last.*
* **Feature/Benefit Blocks (H3)**:
  * **Chart where you work.**
    * Stop carrying clipboards. Chart natively on your phone or iPad with a responsive, one-handed interface.
  * **A Defensible Standard.**
    * Never second-guess your timestamps. Generate pristine, printed PDFs that are instantly legible and easier to stand behind later.
  * **Secure and Offline-Ready.**
    * Designed for office-based sedation. Your records stay encrypted on your device and never sync to a cloud server without your permission.
* **Inline CTA**: Download the app on the App Store.
