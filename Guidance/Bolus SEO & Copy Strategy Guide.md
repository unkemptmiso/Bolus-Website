## 3. Words & Angles to De-emphasize

Avoid sounding like a generic hospital enterprise suite. Bolus wins by being the clean, fast, office-based documentation app.

*   **De-emphasize:** Vague tech language ("AI-powered," "workflow revolution," "next-generation ecosystem"), generic hospital-enterprise terms, "AIMS" as the lead term on the homepage, billing-heavy positioning (if not core), and broad compliance claims that swallow the product story.
*   **Lead with:** Records, charting, speed, clean PDFs, office-based use, and paper replacement.

## 5. Page Building Patterns

Every important page should follow this pattern to satisfy both users and Google:

*   **Page Title:** Clearly reflect the topic. (e.g., *Anesthesia Record App for Office-Based and Mobile Practices | Bolus*)
*   **H1:** Match the main topic directly. (e.g., *Anesthesia Record App Built for Office-Based Care*)
*   **Intro Paragraph:** Immediately explain who it's for, what it does, and why it matters.
*   **H2 Sections:** Use descriptive headers like "Why paper fails," "How Bolus speeds up charting," "Clean PDF export," etc.
*   **Internal Links:** Link naturally to feature pages, audience pages, pricing, and security/HIPAA.

---

## 6. Homepage Strategy

The homepage should target the broad category and push users deeper into specific pages.

*   **Main Targets:** `anesthesia record app`, `office-based anesthesia documentation`, `mobile anesthesia charting`.
*   **Emphasize:** Fast charting, office-based/mobile use, cleaner than paper, PDF export, defensible documentation, designed for specific clinicians, mobile-first workflow.
*   **De-emphasize:** Long legal/compliance lectures, technical architecture, deep feature lists, broad claims about all medical settings, giant blocks of text.

---

## 7. Search Intent & Trust Signals

### Intent Alignment
*   **Product queries** (e.g., "anesthesia record app") → Product page.
*   **Solution queries** (e.g., "office-based anesthesia documentation") → Solution/Use-case page.
*   **Educational queries** (e.g., "how to switch from paper") → Educational article.
*   **Comparison queries** → Comparison page.

### Trust Signals (Healthcare-Adjacent Software)
Ensure the following are visible:
*   Clear company identity & Founder story
*   Clinician-built positioning
*   Clear contact information
*   Privacy Policy, Terms, HIPAA/Security page
*   App Store links & Screenshots
